## True verbs
Verbs in Tamarian are a closed class, and all verbs are intransitive, stative verbs. Non-stative and transitive actions must be expressed using a metaphorical sentence. True verbs can never appear inside a metatopic.

* **bati** *'be open, unfurled'*
* **imati** *'be closed'*
* **arna** *'be tall, standing'*
* **mosa** *'be short, sitting, lying down'*
