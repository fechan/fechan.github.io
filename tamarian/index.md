## Introduction
Tamarian is an alien conlang where all but the most basic ideas and expressions are expressed through idioms. The idioms are rooted in the history and mythology of the Tamarian people, a spacefaring species from Sigma Tama IV in the 23rd Century. The conlang is directly inspired by the [language of the same name](https://memory-alpha.fandom.com/wiki/Tamarian_language) originally introduced in Star Trek: The Next Generation, but which has no canon description other than what appears on screen.

### Goals for this conlang:
1. Derive most expressions from idioms, whether its speakers are aware of its origins or not
2. Be plausible as an everyday language for an alien species
3. Be compatible with the Tamarian language of Star Trek, to at least a moderate degree
4. Explain why a [Universal Translator](https://memory-alpha.fandom.com/wiki/Universal_translator) might fail to translate the language

### Influences
* The central concept of an idiom-based language was inspired by [Tamarian](https://memory-alpha.fandom.com/wiki/Tamarian_language) in Star Trek: The Next Generation.
* The topic-comment structure is inspired by Japanese.
* The classifiers are inspired by [Cantonese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/List_of_Chinese_classifiers), [Japanese](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Japanese_counter_word), and [Navajo](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Navajo_grammar#Classificatory_verbs).
