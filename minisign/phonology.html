Location inventory

The following locations may appear in signs. The hamlrat black square indicates a location to the left or right of a body part; for example, hamlrat,hamhead indicates left of head and hamhead,hamlrat indicates right of head.

    Head and face

  • hamhead Head
  • hamforehead, hamforehead,hamlrat Forehead
  • hameyes Eyes
  • hamear Ear
  • hamnose Nose
  • hamlips, hamlips,hamlrat Lips
  • hamchin Chin
  • hamunderchin Below chin

    Body and other

  • hamneutralspace Neutral space
  • hamneck, hamneck,hamlrat Neck
  • hamlrat,hamshoulders Shoulders
  • hamupperarm Upper arm
  • hamlowerarm Lower arm
  • hamchest hamlrat,hamchest Chest
  • hamstomach Stomach
